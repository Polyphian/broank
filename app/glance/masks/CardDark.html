How To Use

Instructions

1) Open Glance and change theme to User.
2) Download Glance Themer if you dont have it.
3) Tap the download button at the top of this page.
Wait a moment and a pop-up message will appear telling you the theme has been copied. 
4) iOS will automatically run Glance Themer.
5) Tap 'Import Theme' when the menu appears.After the theme has been imported, you'll be asked to activate the theme.
6) Glance Themer will now run Glance with your newly imported theme.
